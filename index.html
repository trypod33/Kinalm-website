Kinalm helps caregiving feel a little less overwhelming.

Kinalm is a private Android app for caregivers and families who are juggling medications, appointments, routines, provider information, health logs, behavior notes, and all the small details that are too easy to lose on a busy day. It brings those pieces together in one calmer place so you can spend less time hunting for information and more time caring for the people who need you.

Why Kinalm was built

I built Kinalm because my own family needed it. I am a caregiver for my two autistic daughters, and I needed a better way to keep track of their appointments and medications alongside my own and my wife’s. What started as something built for our household first has grown into an app I want to share with other caregivers who need something practical, warm, and grounded in real life.

What Kinalm is and what it does

Kinalm is not just a medication reminder or a calendar. It is a caregiving app built to help families keep the practical parts of care together: meds, appointments, provider contacts, health readings, routines, behavior notes, and weekly summaries that are easier to share with doctors, therapists, or schools.

Keep daily care in one place

Track medications, habits, routines, tasks, and health check-ins without jumping between several apps and notebooks.

Stay organized across real family life

Manage care for multiple people, keep provider information handy, and connect appointments and prescribers to the rest of the app.

Share what matters

Use the weekly summary to pull together medication adherence, behaviors, health readings, and this week’s appointments when you need a clearer picture.

Why caregivers are using it

Caregiving often means carrying dozens of small details in your head all day. Kinalm is meant to lower that mental load by making important information easier to enter, easier to find, and easier to share when someone asks, “What happened this week?”

Helpful for real caregiving days

  • Medication schedules and refill awareness.
  • Provider registry for doctors, therapists, and specialists.
  • Calendar appointments with provider context.
  • Behavior and mood tracking when patterns matter.
  • Health logs for things like blood pressure, blood sugar, sleep, weight, and more.

Private and practical

  • Your data stays on the device.
  • Encrypted backup and restore is built in.
  • App lock and PIN protection are available.
  • The APK is shared directly, so you do not need to wait on a Play Store listing.

How to install Kinalm on Android

Because Kinalm is currently shared as a direct Android APK, installation takes a couple of extra steps. Once you do it the first time, it is straightforward.

  1. Tap the Download the APK button on this page.
  2. When your phone finishes downloading, open the APK from your browser, Downloads app, or Files app.
  3. If Android blocks the install, tap the prompt to allow installs from that source, then confirm that you want to continue.
  4. Return to the APK file and tap Install.
  5. When installation finishes, open Kinalm and create your account on the device.
  6. Start by adding the people you care for, then add medications, appointments, providers, and routines.
